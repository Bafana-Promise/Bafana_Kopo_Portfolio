About Bafana Promise

👋 Hi, I'm Bafana Promise Kopo, a Full-Stack JavaScript Developer with over 4 years of experience building scalable web and mobile applications. I enjoy creating clean, efficient, and user-focused solutions using modern JavaScript technologies across the frontend and backend. I'm passionate about solving real-world problems, continuously learning, and delivering software that makes an impact.

From Being a Driver to Developer

My journey into software development began at my local church, where I was introduced to programming by Tukiso Mateka. He took the time to teach me the fundamentals of coding and showed me that a career in software development was possible.

We didn't have access to expensive equipment or ideal learning environments. Resources were limited, and I didn't even own a computer at the time. Despite these challenges, Tukiso encouraged me to keep learning and make the most of what I had.

Inspired by his mentorship, I began learning HTML and CSS using a borrowed laptop and free online resources. I made the most of every opportunity to learn, often studying and practicing whenever I had spare time at work. Every small step brought me closer to achieving my goal of becoming a software developer.

What started as a simple introduction to coding soon grew into a passion and eventually a profession. Today, I have five years of professional experience as a Full-Stack JavaScript Developer, building scalable web and mobile applications. I'm deeply grateful to Tukiso Mateka for believing in me and introducing me to software development, and to everyone else who supported and encouraged me along the way.
