About Bafana Promise

Greetings 👋🏻! I'm Bafana Promise Kopo, a passionate JavaScript full-stack developer with over 4 years of hands-on experience crafting robust and dynamic web and mobile applications. My journey into the world of programming is an unconventional one, driven by sheer determination and a relentless pursuit of knowledge.

From Staff Transporting to Developer

My journey into software development began while working as a staff transport driver, where I spent my days safely transporting employees to and from their destinations. Between trips and during quiet moments, I discovered a passion for coding that would eventually change the course of my career. With only my smartphone and a determination to learn, I immersed myself in online tutorials and documentation, teaching myself the fundamentals of HTML and CSS one lesson at a time. Every spare moment became an opportunity to practice, experiment, and build the foundation that would later grow into a career in software development.

Turning Dreams into Reality

Although I did not have a formal education in computer science, I remained committed to turning my passion for technology into a career. My determination to learn opened the door to an opportunity that would shape my future. I was fortunate to be mentored by **Tukiso Mateka**, who believed in my potential and took the time to guide me through the fundamentals of software development. Under his mentorship, I gained practical knowledge, built confidence in my abilities, and developed the skills needed to grow as a developer. His guidance transformed my passion into a profession and laid the foundation for the career I continue to build today.

A Journey of Growth
